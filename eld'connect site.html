About us

We are three students from the Lycée Albert 1er in Monaco, and through the Change Pact initiative, we have developed a project aimed at fostering connections between generations. Our project focuses on helping seniors connect with volunteers, creating meaningful opportunities for interaction, companionship, and mutual support. By bridging the gap between young volunteers and elderly participants, our project encourages intergenerational exchange, strengthens community bonds, and promotes social inclusion for seniors.
